Basketball

Little Prince Basketball Academy(L.P.B.A) is a professional basketball training organization found in the heart of kibera slums. We run year-round basketball programs including camps, clinics, leagues, teams, tournaments, and training. Our organization was established in the heart of the slums of Kibera, Nairobi-Kenya in 2010. We represent 1 Senior Team Playing in Division II OF the Kenya Basketball Federation.
